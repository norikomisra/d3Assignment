About this infographic

This infographic shows international remittance between countries. The countries sending more money have larger width and each arch is colored based on regions of the countries. Tooltips containing information of remittance amount are available when users hover over country names or chords.

As you can see, the largest amount of remittance is sent from the United State to various countries including Mexico, India, China, Philippines, and Nigeria. To some extent, this may reflect your everyday experience; if you live in the U.S., you may encounter immigrants from these countries more often than those from others. But, how about other countries sending great amount of remittance? Is there any deviation to particular regions? As communication is crucial to live and work, can we see stronger connections between countries in same linguistic areas? You can explore it by manipulating the language filter above.
